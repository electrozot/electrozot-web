REAL EXAMPLE - BOOKING AC REPAIR:

BEFORE:
  1. Select "Electronic Repair" (Category)
  2. Wait for subcategories to load
  3. Select "Major Appliances" (Subcategory)
  4. Wait for services to load
  5. Select "AC Repair" (Service)
  6. Submit
  
  Total Steps: 6
  Total Dropdowns: 3

AFTER:
  1. Select "Major Appliances" (Service Type)
  2. Wait for services to load
  3. Select "AC Repair (Split AC)" (Service with gadget)
  4. Submit
  
  Total Steps: 4
  Total Dropdowns: 2
